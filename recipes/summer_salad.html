Summer Salad

A homemade Italian pasta salad dressing takes this simple pasta salad loaded with veggies, cheese, pepperoni, and olives to a whole new level!
Serve it right away or make it ahead for packed lunches, picnics, or summer barbecues.

Ingredients

For the salad you need:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tri-color rotini pasta
  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tri-color rotini pasta
  • 6 ounces pepperoni sausage, diced
  • 6 ounces pepperoni sausage, diced
  • 1 medium red onion, very thinly sliced and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives, drained
  • ¼ cup minced fresh parsley

For the dressing you need:

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. 1. Cook everything
  2. 2. Mix it all together
